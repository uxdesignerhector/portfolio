La Noche de los Libros 2017

2017 January - April (4 months)

Main role: Support Designer (Intership)

Secondary role: Coordination and support at "Real Casa de Postas"

Summary

Intro

La Noche de los Libros is a big important public event which takes place every year during a weekend before the book day at Madrid, carried by the Autonomous Community of Madrid. Magma was in charge of this event. At the event there are a lot of activities which need to be coordinated and a lot of famous guests as Eduardo Mendoza, the winner of literature "Premio Cervantes 2017", or Irvine Welsh to be attended and also relevant authorities as the president of Community of Madrid.

Context

The project started in January and ended in April, during this time I worked as support designer for Rifle, the design studio hired by Magma for this project. I also worked at the event day working at Real Casas de Postas coordinating parents who brougth theis childrens to the activities and giving support to workshops teams. As every process of design of this magnitude there were constant changes and problems with providers or the client. I'll tell you the problems I faced and how they were solved.

Main poster of the event

Updating the poster

Rifle team asked me to update the old poster to the new dates when the event would take place. The change was simple, I had to change only some text, but the archive I got had all the text converted to curves, that means that I couldn't modify it, it needed to be created again. This was a problem because the previous text had specific characteristics such as kerning, leading, tracking.... With a bit of time and effort I found out which were all the parameters and I could finish my work.

Provider messing with colours

During the impression process there were a problem, the colour didn't correspond with the original colours established by the visual identity. As I had developed the final art all the responsibility fallen at me. That day the supervisor received a call warning her about the problem, then she came to my table and asked me if I were sure if all was correct, I said to her "yes". I was sure because I had revised it various times before sending it. Also, I asked her if she wanted we could revised it, maybe I weren't being aware about a problem in the archive... everything was right. At the end of the day, the problem was solved, it was the printing company fault, they hadn't got the proper pantone colour, so they switch to a similar one expecting Rifle didn't realise about that big change.

Metal Tag

A lot of changes

At the event the team of Magma, external guests and authorities wear a metal tag to indentify them.

Autonomous community of Madrid wanted a bigger logo when the final art was ready to be printed and when we were contacting the provider. Due to it was a small piece every change affected each element of the design breaking also the original logo measures and proportions. There were four changes of this piece till the client were convinced. Notice how the logo of Community of Madrid changed between year 2016 and 2017.

Magma changed of printing and material provider due to the problem with the main poster color we have talked earlier. Creativities that weren't printed needed to be changed to new specified requirements so the metal tag needed to be adjusted again.

Choosing

One day an employee of the printing company presented itself with a lot of diferent metal tags to choose the final one, this time my supervisor was not present so I had to take the decission to choose the correct one, I chose one and that afternoon the printing process started. It was the very first time I had the control of all process from design to production of an important creativity.

Working inside the event

Working at street level

I also worked physically at the event at the "Real Casa de Postas" where activities for children were taking place. I was in charge of the management of children that parents brought to the workshops and I supported the others workshops teams.

By doing this I could see how different was the life from the office to the street level, also I noticed how posters and signage were working.

Problem with queues

There were also a certain problem with people and qeues, there were two events taking place at the same building but with diferent entraces, so queues were mixed up, as workshops had begun and since I was with a low load of work at that moment I decided to go out and warn the people.
