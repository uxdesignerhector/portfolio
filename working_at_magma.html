Working at Magma

2016 | 2017 October - June (9 months)

Main role: Designer (Intership)

Secondary role: IT

Summary

MagmaCultura is a big management company from the cultural sector with more than 900 employees and it has delegations between Madrid, Barcelona and France.

Magma services embrace many projects from different areas, public events as "La Noche de los Libros 2017" (Autonomous Comunity of Madrid) or "Guadalajara y Acción 2017" (Council of Guadalajara), externalized services from museums as the front of Museo Nacional Centro Arte Reina Sofia or CaixaForum. The activities from Fundación la Caixa, Educaixa (El Arte de Educar) at the Prado Museum or the advertisement of Circe Du Soleil in Spain. At the offices we were about thirty people but this number could vary.

My time at the company served me to acquire competence to work in a changing environment where the constant workload never ended and where time was little. The projects concatenated each others so it was easy that I were working for a lot of projects simultaneously with not too much time between them. To solve this, I learnt to use automation in every software and to approach the most efficient way I could and how to organize all my work. As I worked for so many departments from the office, I had to learn to communicate with all kinds of people that not belonged to the design world to understand what they wanted. Magma also served me to work in big important public events were organitation is very complex, such as "La Noche de los Libros 2017" (Madrid).

What did I do at Magma?

  • I developed of all kind of creativities: poster, banners, ads, activities booklets for childs...
  • I created a new image bank and icon bank for Magma
  • I layout contest documents for public or private contest, an important piece for Magma because their economic income depends a lot of them. Intern documents such as dossiers, sale reports and related documents for specifies projects such as Casa Mahou or the photography young contest carried by Fundación Mapfre each year.
  • I worked in big importants events such as "La Noche de los Libros" or "Guadalajara y Acción".
  • I worked as the "computer guy" when the previous computer technician left Magma. I had to solve all kinds of problems so I touched cables, switch boards, routers... while I was receiving orders from Barcelona IT.
  • I worked with Rifle Studio (design team) when they were hired for La Noche de los Libros 2017.
